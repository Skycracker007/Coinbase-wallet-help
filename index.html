Coinbase Wallet Help Center

Getting Started

Coinbase Wallet is a self-custody crypto wallet for managing your digital assets and dApps. It's separate from the Coinbase exchange app.

  1. Download the Coinbase Wallet app from iOS or Android.
  2. Open the app and select Create New Wallet.
  3. Set a passcode and backup your 12-word recovery phrase securely.
⚠️ Coinbase cannot recover your wallet if you lose your recovery phrase.

Access & Security

  • Recovery Phrase: A 12-word key that secures your wallet. Store it offline.
  • Biometric Login: Enable Face ID or fingerprint in Settings > Security.
  • Recover Wallet: On the login screen, tap Restore Wallet and enter your phrase.

Transacting & Tokens

  • Receive: Tap Receive, choose your asset, and share your address or QR code.
  • Send: Tap Send, enter address/amount, confirm, and pay network fees.
  • Supported Assets: ETH, BTC, SOL, MATIC, BNB, and more.

Using dApps & NFTs

  • Access dApps via the in-app browser (Explore tab).
  • NFTs are displayed in the NFTs tab for supported networks.

Troubleshooting

  • Balance shows $0: Confirm you're on the correct network and refresh your wallet.
  • Stuck transactions: Use a blockchain explorer like Etherscan. Retry with a higher gas fee if needed.

Contact & Support

Coinbase Wallet is self-custodial—Coinbase cannot access your funds or recovery phrase.

Need help? Visit the Coinbase Wallet Help Page or go to Settings > Contact Support in the app.

Safety Tips

  • Never share your recovery phrase.
  • Verify wallet addresses before sending.
  • Use caution with unknown links and dApps.
